Private Lessons

Private lessons are a way to help your child receive one-on-one attention that you may be looking for. This is a great way to have your child perfect a skill(s) that they may be struggling with. Private lessons are 30 minutes long and are meant to be taught as a one-on-one session. Semi-private lessons are available with a $10 discount per child. Children should be of like swimming abilities when booking semi-private lessons.
