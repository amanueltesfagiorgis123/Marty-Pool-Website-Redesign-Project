Important Information
Make sure to bring a towel and goggles for each swimmer (goggles are not required for Parent Toddler lessons). We allow swimmers onto the pool deck 5 minutes before the start of their lesson.
Make Up/Credit for missed classes: When you sign up for group classes, you are committing to the entire session. We cannot accommodate single day transfers to convenience your schedule. Classes will not be pro-rated for classes missed. There will be no credit or refund for group classes not cancelled by the Mary Wayte staff. If you must cancel a private lesson, there must be at least 24-hour notice to receive a credit or refund.
Group Lesson Session Dates: Our 2025 Group Lesson Calendar is available by CLICKING HERE. You will find open registration dates and the dates of our sessions here. Our calendar is subject to change without notice. Please check it regularly.
